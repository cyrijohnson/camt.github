About Us

After much prayer and consideration, Comforter Missions U.S. was established in 2005, as a sister organization to Comforer Action Mission Trust (CAMT) in India which began in 1994. Comforter Missions is a non-profit 501 (c) 3 organization registered in Maine.

David Stanly

David Stanly leads Comforter Missions U.S. in close connection with his brother James Raj in India. David and his wife Martha live in South Portland, Maine, with their three sons Calvin,Victor and Wesley.

James Raj

Comforter Missions is a ministry organization that caters to the physical, emotional and spiritual needs of the people. We aim to improve the quality of life by reaching affectionately, caring compassionately, and helping effectively. Inspired by the teachings of Jesus, we provide basic necessities like food, water, shelter, clothing, and education to the poor and needy.
